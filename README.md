# lettre

## Category
- Digital Forensics
- OSINT

## Difficulty
Easy–Medium

## Description

A close friend reported that someone disappeared without a trace shortly after sending what appeared to be an ordinary letter.

Authorities found no signs, no further messages, and no known destination. The only item recovered was a single image containing the letter.

Your task is to examine and uncover every hidden piece of evidence, and determine where the missing person was trying to lead you.

Nothing is ever hidden in just one place.

**Flag format:** 
`flag{----------_------}`

First part of flag is the missing person's job all in lower caps and no spaces, second part is the release date in format ddmmyy.
